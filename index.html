Afya Innovation Lab

An innovation space, set to transform the healthcare technology sector in Kenya and beyond.

The healthcare sector is constantly evolving, and the need for innovation has never been greater.

Located at the University of Nairobi Faculty of Health Sciences, the lab provides a dedicated platform for medical students and healthcare enthusiasts alike to collaborate, innovate, and create solutions that will drive positive change in the healthcare system.

It is designed to foster an enabling environment that promotes creativity, collaboration, and impactful innovation in healthcare.
